Who We Are

Founded in 2010 by visionary entrepreneur Souvik Patra, Dreams Become True began as a small team of travel enthusiasts with a shared dream: to transform how people experience the world. What started as a passion project in Kolkata has blossomed into a global travel platform, helping thousands of travelers discover hidden gems and create unforgettable memories.

Today, we're proud to have grown into a family of over 50 travel experts, photographers, writers, and local guides across six continents, all dedicated to crafting extraordinary journeys that go beyond the ordinary.

Souvik Patra

Founder & CEO

A passionate traveler and entrepreneur, Souvik started Dreams Become True with a simple belief: that travel has the power to transform lives. His vision has guided the company from its humble beginnings to becoming an industry leader in experiential travel.

"I wanted to create more than just a travel company - I wanted to build a platform that helps people discover not just new places, but new perspectives about the world and themselves."
